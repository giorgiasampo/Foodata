DescriptionThe dataset contains the production different kinds of food in Europe (27 Countries). List of food selected: cheese, whole cow milk; eggs, hen, in shell; meat, cattle; meat, chicken; meat, pig; milk, whole fresh cow; potatoes; rice, paddy; wheat.The dataset contains demographic information about the average lifespan at birth based on sex and geographic location within the EU from 2010-2019. The dataset uses 27 NUTS 0 countries (EU member states) and 302 NUTS 1 and 2 regions.The datasets contains data relative to agricultural are employed for low, medium and high input farms, soil erosion due to agriculture and forestry, irrigable and irrigated land, greenhouse gas emissions due to agriculture and farming, air pollution and water pollution per country.
