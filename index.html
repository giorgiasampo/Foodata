FOOData: food in data

This project aims at showing connections between food production, pollution and health conditions in European countries from 2009 to 2019.

We are focusing in particular on different countries production of food and their health and pollution status

To do so we collected data from EUROSTAT, the European Environmental Agency and FAO.
